    Services

    Service 1

    The flat serve is perhaps one of the most sought-after serves in tennis because its synonymous with power and the ability to hit an ace.

    Service 2

    The slice serve is a highly effective serve that can become a huge weapon when perfected. What makes the slice serve unique is the sidespin applied, which causes the ball to move from right to left (if youre a righty) and continue to move in that direction off the bounce.

    Service 3

    Of all the serves, the kick serve is arguably the most valuable and highly sought after for players to learn. However, its often one of the most challenging serves to develop.
